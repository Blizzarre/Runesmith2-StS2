## Runesmith 2 2026-04-30 Changelog

### Balances
- Ice-Cold now reduces by half amount at the end of turn (instead of 1).
  - Note: Ice-Cold is very strong because you can outpace the stack depletion very easily. By halving it instead, you will find it harder to keep up with the reduction.
- Adjusted numbers of cards that apply Ice-Cold to take into account the change in reduction.
  - Frozen Chisel: Apply 3(4) ⇒ 4(6) Ice-Cold.
  - Gelid Canister: Apply 4(5) ⇒ 4(6) Ice-Cold.
  - Reduce Entropy: Gain 11(15) ⇒ 8(12) Block. Apply 4(5) ⇒ 6(8) Ice-Cold. Gain 2 ⇒ 4 Ice-Cold.
    - This is changed so that the numbers looks different from the other cards. To balance it, you gain more Ice-Cold in return.
  - Glacies (Rune): Apply 1 ⇒ 2 Ice-Cold. 
- Adamantium Hammer: 10(14) damage ⇒ 5(7) damage
  - Adamantium Hammer is really strong because it receives Enhance from every source. This is toned down for now but there may be rework if it's still too strong.
- Fulgor: Craft with 6(8) Potency ⇒ 4(6) Potency.
